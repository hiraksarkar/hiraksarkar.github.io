--- layout: null section-type: bio title: Brief-Bio --- ### Brief Bio I received my and masters degree from Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) in 2013 and batchelors degree from Kalyani Govt. Engineering College in 2011, both in computer science. At ISI, I defended my masters thesis in graph theory under the supervision of Prof. Bhargab Bhattacharyya and Prof. Sandip Das. Before joining Stony Brook, I had spent some time at National University of Singapore as a researcher. I also worked as a research fellow at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. During a brief period I was a visiting researcher at Indian Statistical Institute, at the same time I served as a guest lecturer at computer science department, Vivekananda University. I had the great fortune to briefly work with Swami Sarvottamanandaji, fondly known as Sreesh Maharaj.
